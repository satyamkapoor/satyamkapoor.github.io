Satyam Kapoor

            

Hi there! I’m Satyam Kapoor—a tech enthusiast with a global perspective. Born and raised in India, I’ve spent two-thirds of my life there before moving to Europe. Today, I call Frankfurt home, but I consider myself a citizen of the world.

As a Site Reliability Engineer, I’m passionate about building reliable systems and exploring the latest engineering innovations that make a real impact on people’s lives. Whether it’s streamlining workflows, enhancing security, or making technology more accessible, I love discovering solutions that help others achieve more.

I write about technology, home automation, DevOps, and the internet on my blog, and for a more personal perspective, I share stories from my daily adventures and experiences in my journal.
